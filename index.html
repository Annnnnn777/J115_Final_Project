Sliced Pork Soup

Materials: pork, green onion, ginger, starch, salt, soy sauce, tomato(optional), mushroom(optional), other vegetable(optional)

1. Pork Preparation: cut into thin slices, and marinate with salt, soy sauce, oil, and starch. Let it sit for ten minutes.

2. Add oil, green onion, and ginger into the pot. After that, add tomato and fry until it becomes soft. You can also put in any other vegetable you like. Then put in water.

3. After the water is boiled, add the pork.
